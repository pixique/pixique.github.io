Common questions

Who is "we"?

The install is led by Bea Katcher. Specialist collaborators are engaged as needed for tool-specific build/config. Your team owns the system post-handoff.

Do you replace our tools?

No. We build in your stack (Notion, Asana, Monday, Airtable). Mid-migration? We use wrappers and freeze points so teams keep working.

Will people actually follow the process?

We install constraint-driven systems (SLAs, WIP limits, QA gates) and dashboards that create behavior — not just docs that collect dust.

How is success measured?

We baseline KPIs in kickoff and review weekly. If two targets aren't met by Day 30, we extend at no cost.

Can you stay on retainer?

We offer optional advisory office hours ($3–5K/mo) and an SLA warranty ($1.5–2.5K/mo), but the system is built to run without us.

What if our scope changes mid-project?

We lock scope at kickoff and manage a change log. Material changes roll into an add-on or follow-on sprint so we protect timelines and outcomes.
