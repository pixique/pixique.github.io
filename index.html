Security & Confidentiality

Your data stays yours.

We work inside enterprise systems every day. Here's how we handle access, confidentiality, and ownership.

How do you access our systems?

You grant access to your project management tools via your standard contractor onboarding process. We use your SSO where available. Access is revoked at engagement end—no lingering credentials.

What data do you see?

Project metadata: timelines, assignments, status, briefs. We don't need access to financials, HR systems, or customer PII. If sensitive campaigns exist, we can work around them.

We work with 2 clients at a time, never in the same vertical simultaneously. We sign mutual NDAs before any diagnostic work begins.

You do. Every deliverable—documents, templates, tool configurations, training recordings—transfers to you completely. No licensing fees. No ongoing dependencies.

Yes. Professional liability and errors & omissions coverage. Certificate of insurance available on request during contracting.

Yes. We're set up for enterprise contracting—MSAs, SOWs, vendor onboarding forms, security questionnaires. We've done this before.
